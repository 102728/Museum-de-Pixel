The doom guy

The Doom Guy is a space marine controlled by the player, fighting demons with weapons like a pistol, shotgun, and BFG 9000. Known for his green armor and helmet, he's a silent protagonist who doesn't speak during the game.

The Zombieman

The Zombieman is a former human turned into a zombie by the demons. They are the weakest enemies in the game, armed with a pistol. They are slow and easy to kill.
